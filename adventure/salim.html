About Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary:

The Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary, also known as the Thattekad Bird Sanctuary, is a renowned bird sanctuary located in the southern Indian state of Kerala. It is named after the famous Indian ornithologist Dr. Salim Ali and is known for its rich avian biodiversity. The sanctuary was established in 1983 and is named in honor of Dr. Salim Ali, who was a pioneer in the field of ornithology in India. Dr. Salim Ali played a significant role in advocating for the conservation of bird habitats in India, and this sanctuary stands as a tribute to his contributions.

History:

The Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary was established in 1983 and is named in honor of Dr. Salim Ali, who was a pioneer in the field of ornithology in India. Dr. Salim Ali played a significant role in advocating for the conservation of bird habitats in India, and this sanctuary stands as a tribute to his contributions.

Significance:

  • Biodiversity: The sanctuary is known for its exceptional avian diversity, with over 300 species of birds recorded here. It's considered one of the richest bird habitats in peninsular India.
  • Endangered Species: The sanctuary is home to several endangered and vulnerable bird species, making it a vital conservation area.
  • Research and Education: The sanctuary serves as a research and education center for bird enthusiasts, ornithologists, and students interested in studying and conserving birds.

Specialties:

  • Birdwatching: Birdwatchers and wildlife enthusiasts flock to the Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary for the opportunity to observe a wide variety of bird species, including kingfishers, hornbills, woodpeckers, and migratory birds.
  • Scenic Beauty: Apart from birds, the sanctuary offers serene and picturesque landscapes, with lush greenery, rivers, and small streams.
  • Butterflies and Insects: The sanctuary is also home to diverse butterfly and insect species, making it a delightful place for insect enthusiasts.
  • Nature Walks: Visitors can explore the sanctuary through guided nature walks and birding trails, offering a close encounter with its natural beauty.

Nearby Places:

  • Bhoothathankettu Dam: This dam is located nearby and is a popular spot for picnics and boat rides.
  • Kothamangalam: Kothamangalam is the nearest town to the sanctuary and offers accommodations, restaurants, and shopping options.
  • Munnar: The hill station of Munnar is not far from the Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary and is a renowned tourist destination known for its tea plantations and scenic beauty.
  • Ernakulam: The city of Ernakulam, part of the greater Kochi area, is a major urban center in Kerala and offers various cultural and recreational activities.

Location:

The Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary is situated at approximately 15.4771° N latitude and 73.7913° E longitude in the state of Kerala, India.
